School Acheivments

Bhavans International School Quiz- BISQ 2020

The final event- "Bhavans Inter-School Online Quiz-BISQ was held virtually. There were 6 teams that participated in the quiz from different countries across the Gulf. The competition was very tough and challenging. Students of BIS were extraordinarly confident; they surprised the audience with their in-depth knowledge and information and proved themselves as a genius by emerging as the winners of BISQ, winning the First prize of- 1000 USD. BIS family proudly congratulates the team!!!

Award Ceremony for SOF winners

It was a joyous moment for grade 3 and 4 students as they were awarded medals and certificates for their outstanding performance in the Olympiads. Congratulations to all the children and thier parents too!

Acheivment award certificate

"The harder you work for something, the greater you feel when you achieve it." Students of Grades 1-3 of academic year 2018-19 recieved Outstanding Achievements certificates for scoring excellant marks.
